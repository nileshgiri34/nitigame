Spotlight on Indian Gaming Creators

Celebrating the creators who shape India’s gaming community.

Meet the Stars

India’s gaming scene has exploded over the last few years, and with it a new generation of content creators who entertain and inspire millions. From streaming intense battle royale matches to producing engaging vlogs and tutorials, these creators are at the heart of our community. Here are a few you should check out:

Mortal

One of India’s most beloved professional gamers and streamers, Mortal (Naman Mathur) is known for his skill in titles like PUBG Mobile and BGMI. He co‑founded S8UL Esports and regularly streams gameplay, tutorials and team events.

Follow him on YouTube and Instagram.

Dynamo Gaming

Aaditya "Dynamo" Sawant started by posting humorous GTA videos before becoming one of India’s biggest livestreamers. He is known for his engaging personality and high‑intensity gameplay streams of BGMI and PUBG Mobile.

Watch him on YouTube and join his Instagram.

Total Gaming (Ajju Bhai)

Total Gaming, known as Ajju Bhai, is one of the most subscribed Indian gaming creators. He posts videos and streams of Garena Free Fire, known for his tips, tricks and entertaining commentary.

Subscribe on YouTube and follow him on Instagram.

These are just a few of the many talented creators in India. Follow them for entertainment and inspiration—and keep an eye on this section for more features soon!
